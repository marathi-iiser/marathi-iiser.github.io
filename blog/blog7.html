Omkarotsav 2020, IISER Pune

...

Summary:

    Event :              Omkarotsav 2020
    Date :               22nd Aug - 1st Sept
    Coordinators: Shruti Patil, Pranav Sadhu, Atharva Bhide(All 2nd years)
    Sub-Events :
    1. Inauguration of My Marathi Club’s Website
    2. Competition
    3. Decoration Competition
    4. Omkarotsav Meet
    5. Mythology Hunt
    6. Culture Programme

Brief Account of Events:
  1. Inauguration of Website:
    2. 22nd Aug: The Website of Marathi club, made from scratch by the volunteers was released on this auspicious day.

  2. Art competition:
    3. Collaborating with the art club, art competition was held. Theme of the arts was Lord Ganesha. We received some great pieces of art. Harshita Patnaik from 2nd year won the competition.



  3. Decoration competition:
    4. In this competition participants were supposed to send pics or videos of their Ganesha decoration at home. All the decorations were so great that we weren’t able to decide the winner.

  4. Omkarostav meet:
    5. This was a fun event, we gathered on google meet and students shared about Omkarotsav celebrations in their states

  5. Mythology hunt:
    6. We collaborated with iplug and made a conquistar with the theme of mythologies around the world. Participation was in good numbers and it was a great experience.

  6. Cultural Programme:
    7. In this event participants were asked to send dance and/or song performance themed Ganesha. We received 6 wonderful performances.Do see the youtube video.

Do visit our instagram page to see photos and videos of all the competitions and much more.
Omkarotsav is a festival we all love, and we had to get creative this year. Thanks to hardworking coordinators and volunteers and wonderful participants, we were able to pull off a spectacular omkarotsav!

-Vinayak Pendse,
(volunteer, My Marathi mandal, Batch 2024)
