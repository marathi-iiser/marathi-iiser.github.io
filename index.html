    About Us

    This is a platform for Marathi speaking as well as non-Marathi people on the campus. We would like to make it all known and appreciated, this city as well as it's perks. You need a good trek, we will help you with our best resources, even organize them frequently. Need to satisfy your tummy after those not so good quizzes? We have got you covered here too. We will recommend all the classic eateries that even your Zomato doesn't know. And you can give your order in the native language.

    Activities

    Our major activities for the upcoming year

    Colloquial Marathi Classes

    We give you the opportunity to learn basic Marathi from scratch. If you know some basics, you can still understand advanced language skills. This will help you to communicate with locals.

    Exploring Pune

    We plan to arrange trips around the city to see local heritage sites, museums and enjoy local Maharashtrian cuisines.

    Meet for Marathi

    These is specifically meant for but not limited to people who know the Marathi language. We will be exploring Marathi literature, culture, the language here. This will include poetry recitals, book discussions, word puzzles, and many more such activities.

    Omkar Utsav

    Ganesh festival is the most attractive event in Pune and it's also celebrated in IISER. Many departments like decoration, feast, logistic work together to make it a success in eco friendly way.
